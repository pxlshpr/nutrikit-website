🎬 Theme Switching Test

How to Test Video Theme Switching:

  1. Open http://localhost:3001 in another tab
  2. Scroll down to the scanner section
  3. Let the video start playing
  4. Use the buttons on this page to switch themes:
    • Click "Toggle Theme" or "Dark Mode"
    • The video should instantly switch to the dark theme version
    • Click "Light Mode" to switch back
  5. Notice: The video continues playing, just switches to the other theme

Alternative: Use System Preferences

macOS:

  1. Open System Preferences → General → Appearance
  2. Switch between Light and Dark
  3. Watch the video instantly switch themes!

Windows:

  1. Settings → Personalization → Colors
  2. Choose your color mode
  3. Video will switch automatically

What Should Happen:

  • ✅ Video switches from scanner-light.webm to scanner-dark.webm
  • ✅ Playback continues smoothly (minimal interruption)
  • ✅ Poster image also updates to match theme
  • ✅ Works for both WebM and MP4 formats
